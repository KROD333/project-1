MUSE - Time Is Running Out

About the Song

"Time Is Running Out" is a song by British rock band MUSE, released in 2003 as part of their album Absolution. The song is known for its intense vocals and thought-provoking lyrics, and like many of their other songs in the album, is often masked and portrayed as unhealthy and obsessive relationships.

About MUSE

MUSE is a British alternative rock band known for their cinematic and grandiose style, consisting of Matthew Bellamy (guitarist, keyboardist, singer, songwriter), Christopher Wolstenholme (bassist, background vocalist), and Dominic Howard (drummer), that formed at their school, in 1994.
